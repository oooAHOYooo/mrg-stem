What You'll Need

  • A computer with Windows 10/11, Mac, or Linux
  • At least 8GB of RAM (16GB recommended)
  • A graphics card that supports DirectX 11 or 12
  • About 20GB of free storage space

Step-by-Step Installation

Step 1: Create an Epic Games Account

  1. Go to www.epicgames.com
  2. Click "Sign In" in the top right corner
  3. Click "Sign Up" to create a new account
  4. Fill in your information (you may need a parent's help)
  5. Verify your email address

Step 2: Download Epic Games Launcher

  1. Visit www.unrealengine.com
  2. Click "Download Now" button
  3. Download the Epic Games Launcher installer
  4. Run the installer and follow the on-screen instructions
  5. Sign in with your Epic Games account

Step 3: Install Unreal Engine 5

  1. Open the Epic Games Launcher
  2. Click on the "Unreal Engine" tab at the top
  3. Click the "Install Engine" button
  4. Choose a location to install (make sure you have enough space!)
  5. Select "Unreal Engine 5.0" or the latest version
  6. Click "Install" and wait for it to download (this may take 30-60 minutes)

Step 4: Verify Installation

  1. Once installed, you should see "Launch" button next to Unreal Engine 5
  2. Click "Launch" to test that everything works
  3. If it opens successfully, you're ready to go! üéâ

Troubleshooting

Installation is slow? This is normal! UE5 is a large program (about 20GB)

Can't find the launcher? Check your Downloads folder or Desktop

Need help? Ask your teacher or visit the Epic Games support page
