How to Download and Remix Locally:

  1. Click on any template link above to open it on Scratch
  2. Click the "See inside" button or "Remix" button
  3. Go to File → Save to your computer to download the .sb3 file
  4. Open the Scratch Desktop app on your computer
  5. Go to File → Load from your computer and select your downloaded .sb3 file
  6. Now you can edit and remix the project locally on your computer!
