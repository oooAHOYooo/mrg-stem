Story + Game Prompts for Scratch

1. Make a Character With a Goal

What does your character want? How will they move to get it?

  • A dog chasing a bone
  • A robot looking for batteries
  • A squirrel grabbing acorns before winter

2. Make a World With One Rule

Create a world and give it one rule. What happens if you break it?

  • In space you float unless you press a button
  • In a haunted house you move slow until you find a flashlight
  • In a jungle you cannot touch snakes

3. Make a Reaction Moment

When you do something, the game reacts.

  • Press space and your character jumps or yells
  • Click a ghost and it disappears
  • Touch a magic item and unlock a power

4. Build a 10-Second Challenge

Make a tiny game that lasts 10 seconds. Something moves. You have a goal.

  • Catch falling stars
  • Dodge bouncing balls
  • Race to a treasure chest

5. Create Talking Moments

Make something talk when you interact with it.

  • Click the wizard to get a quest
  • Walk near a villager and they talk to you
  • Touch a sign to see a clue

How to Download and Remix Locally:

  1. Click on any template link above to open it on Scratch
  2. Click the "See inside" button or "Remix" button
  3. Go to File → Save to your computer to download the .sb3 file
  4. Open the Scratch Desktop app on your computer
  5. Go to File → Load from your computer and select your downloaded .sb3 file
  6. Now you can edit and remix the project locally on your computer!
